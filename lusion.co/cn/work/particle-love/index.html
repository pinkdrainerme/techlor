Particle Love

Take Realtime Particle Effects to a whole new level.

[此案例未有提供中文翻譯]

Particle Love started as an internal project during our down time just for fun! It is a series of particle experiments using different rendering techniques. Much to our surprise, it became the source of reference of plenty of our client’s projects, so we kept adding more and more experiments into the collection, because of how well Particle Love was received. It won a SOTD on FWA and some of our particle experiments were featured on Chrome Experiments, 3D Web Fest, Google I/O etc. We also somehow became the ‘go to guy’ for all sorts of particle-related projects!

The collection of Particle Love includes the following particle experiments:

  • Touch with Leap Motion
  • Constraints
  • The Spirit
  • Icicle Bubbles
  • Hyper Mix
